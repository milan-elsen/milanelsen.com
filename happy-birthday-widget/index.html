Never forget a birthday again!

Happy Birthday Widget lets you see birthdays right on your Home Screen. Happy Birthday Widget comes with a variety of themes and sizes to fit your personal style.

Happy Birthday Widget

iOS + iPadOS

Widgets

Choose from three different Widgets:
- List: S, M, L - Photo: S, M, L - Countdown: S, M

Customisation

Choose from a variety of Themes to fit your style: Black, Candy, Dusk, Lightning, Midnight, Ocean, Peach, Sunflower & more

Seamless

Happy Birthday Widget integrates with your Contacts Database, so there is no setup required. Your favourite Widgets will be up and running in no time.

Private & Secure

No ads, no tracking. Your Contacts stay on device. In fact Happy Birthday Widget does not collect any data at all, not even analytics.

Free

Happy Birthday Widget is totally free. If you enjoy the app you can tip the developer to support future development.
