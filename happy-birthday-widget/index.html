Happy Birthday Widget


Never forget a birthday again. Happy Birthday Widget lets you see them right on your Home Screen. Happy Birthday Widgets comes with a variety of themes and sizes to fit your style and home screen aesthetic.

Widgets

Choose from three different Widgets:
- List: S, M, L
- Photo: S, M, L
- Countdown: S, M

Customize

Choose from a variety of Themes to fit your style:
Black, Candy, Dusk, Lightning, Midnight, Ocean, Peach, Petrol, Pine, Stone, Sunflower & Terminal

Seamless

Happy Birthday Widget integrates with your Contacts Database, so there is no setup required. Your favourite Widgets will be up and running in a breeze. Widgets come in a variety of sizes to take full advantage of your Home Screen.

Secure & Private

No ads, no tracking. Your Contacts stay on device. In fact Happy Birthday Widget does not collect any data at all, not even analytics.

Free

Happy Birthday Widget is totally free. If you enjoy the app you can tip the developer to support future development.
