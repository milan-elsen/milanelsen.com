MILAN ELSEN

As a multidisciplinary designer in Germany, I am interested in the intersection of creativity and technology, and I have a great passion for fashion, aesthetics, pop culture, and human-machine interactions. I am fascinated by how all these areas converge in design. To this end, I have a solid foundation in app development and UI/UX design, where I have gained practical experience as an independent iOS developer and interface designer.
Through my interest in art and philosophy, I have developed a highly conceptual and integrated design approach at the Cologne International School of Design, where I have been studying since 2023. My focus is on interaction design in the context of exhibition pieces, as well as design in the cultural and exhibition sector. Currently I am on exchange at PolyU School of Design in Hong Kong.

Open for select collaboration in interaction design and exhibition concepts.
